Economic Feasibility

For 100 acres


___________________________________
The cost of the cultivation and production process

- Cost of the device: 80,000 EGP per 100 acres (100 devices).
- Sprinkler irrigation system: price 10,000 EGP per 100 acres
- Workers' salary: one worker for every 100 feddans, with a salary of 72,000 EGP per year.
- Quantity and price of irrigation water: (from 2500 cubic meters to 3500 cubic meters) (from 3 million liters to 4 million liters) (from 500 EGP to 600 EGP) per 100 feddans per year
- Fertilizer: an average of 100 sulphate kg phosphate per year per 100 acres, at a price (average of 5000 EGP fertilizer)
Final cost:the average cost of the irrigation process + the average cost of the cultivation process, production and distribution without irrigation (the average cost of cultivation was answered by one of the professors in the Faculty of Agriculture, Tanta University) = 160,000 EGP + 1,000,000 EGP = 1,160,000 EGP for 100 acres
___________________________________
Financial return:

Ardab: the number of the produced crop (900 ardab per 100 acres), the price of one ardab (4000 EGP)
- Feddan: the number of the produced crop (100 feddans), the price of one feddan (36000 EGP)
The final financial return: 3,600,000 EGP for 100 acres
___________________________________
Profits:

Profits: = final financial return - final cost = 3,600,000 EGP - 1,160,000 EGP = 2,440,000 EGP per 100 acres
Profits ratio: = (profit / cost price) x 100% = (2,440,000 EGP / 800,000 EGP) x 100% = 305%
